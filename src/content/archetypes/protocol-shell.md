Protocol Shells work best when the table treats them as people under pressure, not just as robots with immunity notes. Their drama lives where programming, personhood, and ka start overlapping.

## What you bring to the table

- Durable utility with strong old-tech flavor
- A nonhuman perspective that can be funny, tragic, or eerie
- Excellent hooks for ruins, relics, and the ethics of the age before

## Best fit

Choose the Protocol Shell if you want a character who is half artifact, half survivor, and fully forced to answer whether purpose can evolve into soul.
