Walkers are the class for players who want to treat the map like an argument instead of a fact. They make the party safer, stranger, and more capable of surviving when reality stops behaving.

## What you bring to the table

- Route control, scouting, and escape valves
- Cosmic weirdness without becoming a pure spellcaster
- A strong role in exploration-heavy and horror-heavy sessions

## What makes the class sing

The Walker shines when the group is cornered, lost, or crossing worlds. If a campaign leans on doors, pursuit, strange geography, or time wounds, you will feel essential.
