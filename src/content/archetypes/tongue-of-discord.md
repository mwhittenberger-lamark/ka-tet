Tongues of Discord are at their best when they complicate scenes without stealing every scene. They should feel dangerous because they can change what people believe, not because they remove all uncertainty from conversation.

## What you bring to the table

- Powerful social and psychic control
- Strong infiltration and interrogation play
- A class fantasy that turns language into a real battlefield

## Best fit

Pick the Tongue of Discord if you want your character's most memorable victories to start with a sentence nobody in the room can quite forget.
