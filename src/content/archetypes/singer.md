Singers turn the emotional tone of a scene into mechanics. They are equal parts healer, mystic, performer, and truth-serum for a world held together by resonant force.

## What you bring to the table

- Flexible support and battlefield control
- Big emotional spotlight scenes
- Strong links to prophecy, faith, and the Beam

## Best fit

Choose the Singer if you want your class fantasy to affect both the fight and the fiction around it. A Singer can heal the group, terrify a room, or turn one lyric into a campaign-defining omen.
