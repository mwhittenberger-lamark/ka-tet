Demon-Blooded characters are strongest when the table treats their power as both a gift and a pressure point. They should feel useful, frightening, and never entirely safe.

## What you bring to the table

- High drama and volatile offensive magic
- Strong resistance and comeback tools under pressure
- A built-in moral fault line the story can keep testing

## Best fit

Choose the Demon-Blooded if you want the kind of character who can save the day in a terrible way and then has to live with how easy that felt.
