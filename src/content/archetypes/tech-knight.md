Tech-Knights carry the torch of science in a dark age. They salvage, decode, modify, and weaponize relics no sane villager wants to touch twice.

## What you bring to the table

- Strong utility in ruins, bunkers, and old-world sites
- Gear fixes that keep the group moving under pressure
- A martial-caster hybrid feel without becoming a traditional wizard

## Best fit

Pick the Tech-Knight if you want to be the ka-tet member everyone turns to when a door locks, a gun jams, a robot wakes up, or a ruined control panel starts speaking in static.
