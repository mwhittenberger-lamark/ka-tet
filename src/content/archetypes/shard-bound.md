Shard-Bound characters are strongest when the player enjoys making the internal cast feel distinct without slowing the table down. The fun lives in contrast, choice, and the moment one shard knows exactly what another would never allow.

## What you bring to the table

- Flexible role coverage that can shift scene by scene
- Strong resistance to psychic and possession-based threats
- Some of the richest roleplay hooks in the entire class list

## Best fit

Choose the Shard-Bound if you want the most psychologically complex archetype on the site and you enjoy turning internal conflict into visible tactical strength.
