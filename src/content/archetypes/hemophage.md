Hemophages are strongest when the table treats hunger as pressure, not as an excuse to derail trust. The tension should live in choice, scarcity, and what the character is willing to do to stay human.

## What you bring to the table

- Fast, personal, self-sustaining melee pressure
- Strong horror flavor without needing full villainy
- A recurring survival cost that keeps the roleplay sharp

## Best fit

Choose the Hemophage if you want every victory to ask what it cost your body, your ethics, or your promise to the rest of the tet.
