Touch-Bearers give the game a clean way to make ka audible. They are often the party member who notices what the world is trying to say before anyone else is ready to hear it.

## What you bring to the table

- Emotional and psychic intelligence that changes scenes before combat starts
- Strong support tools built around warning, linking, and insight
- A natural channel for visions, omens, and Tower-facing mysteries

## Best fit

Pick the Touch-Bearer if you want a character who is powerful precisely because they cannot stay numb to what others ignore.
