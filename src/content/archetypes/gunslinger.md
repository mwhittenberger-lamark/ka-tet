Gunslingers are the quickest answer to chaos. They are not just marksmen, but symbols: oath-bearers from a world that moved on, still trying to prove their code matters.

## What you bring to the table

- Strong opening rounds and duel pressure
- Reliable cover fire and bodyguard play
- Moral gravity in scenes where mercy, order, and revenge collide

## Why players choose this path

If you want a class that feels iconic the moment it enters a room, the Gunslinger is the cleanest fit. You carry danger openly and still have to decide what kind of legend you become.
