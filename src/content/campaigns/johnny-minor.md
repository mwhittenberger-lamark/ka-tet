Johnny Minor is the kind of campaign that works best when players want to care deeply, argue honestly, and keep moving anyway.

## Why it works as a flagship campaign

It showcases almost every pillar of the setting at once: the Tower, the Beams, the strange roads between worlds, and a ka-tet trying to decide whether prophecy deserves obedience.
