Ka Points are the signature pressure valve of the game. They let the table say, "this moment matters enough for fate to lean on it."

## What Ka does best

- Smooths over a brutal streak of bad luck
- Rewards players who reinforce the party bond
- Gives weird, desperate scenes a mythic push

## Earning Ka

The GM should hand out Ka when players honor promises, choose sacrifice over ease, lean into destiny, or pull each other through impossible situations.

## Dark Ka

Groups that want a sharper horror edge can track Dark Ka separately. It buys power fast, but every use should leave a stain.
