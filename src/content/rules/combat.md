Combat in Ka-Tet should feel mean, cinematic, and brief. The system rewards planning, positioning, and nerve more than long attrition grinds.

## Principles

- Violence escalates quickly.
- Cover is life.
- Fear, panic, and collateral damage are always on the table.

If a scene feels like a stand-up slugfest, add movement, civilians, environment danger, or a clock.
