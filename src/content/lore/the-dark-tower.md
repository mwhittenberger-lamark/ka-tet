The Dark Tower is not just a place. It is the axis of reality, the mythic center around which worlds, stories, and consequences all turn.

In the book's cosmology, the Tower stands at the center of all existence. If it falls, order gives way to Discordia and the worlds collapse into chaos. Most people in play will never see it, but their lives are still shaped by it because every Beam, prophecy, and strange road points back to the same impossible center.

Players do not need every answer on day one. They only need to understand that the Tower matters because all worlds lean on it, whether they know it or not.
