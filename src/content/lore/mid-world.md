Mid-World is the emotional baseline of the setting: half frontier elegy, half collapsing myth. It is where old codes still matter because so much else has failed.

This is Roland Deschain's world, but it is bigger than any one gunslinger story. The land holds farming Callas, haunted roads, ruined cities like Lud, broken kingdoms, old rail lines, wastelands, and strange seams where time and distance stop behaving properly.

Life in Mid-World mixes western frontier survival with the remains of lost high technology and older magic. One day you may camp beside a mule trail; the next you are picking your way through Old Ones machinery or listening for a thinny in the dark.
