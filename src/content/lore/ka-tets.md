A ka-tet is not only a party. It is a promise the universe appears to have made before the characters understood it.

The phrase means "one from many." A ka-tet is a group bound together by destiny for a purpose, even if nobody knows that purpose yet. The bond is practical as much as mystical: you share the road, the danger, the water, the guilt, and the hope.

That is why so much of the game's tone depends on the group acting like a tet instead of a collection of loners. The rules support that bond, but the heart of it is in the choices players make for each other.
