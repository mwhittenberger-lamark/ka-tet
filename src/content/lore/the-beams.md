The Beams are often described as lines, but players tend to understand them faster as pressure, harmony, and balance. When the Beams are wounded, the world starts to sound wrong before it looks wrong.

The book describes six great Beams stretching from the Dark Tower like spokes of a wheel. Each one anchors reality across worlds and ends at paired portals guarded by ancient beings. Along a Beam, people with the right sensitivity may feel a hum, hear a song, or receive a prophetic nudge they cannot explain.

They matter because the Crimson King's servants are trying to break them. Every tremor, omen, and failing place in the setting lives under that larger threat, whether the party knows it yet or not.
