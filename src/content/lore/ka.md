Ka is easiest to grasp through examples. It is the reason certain people meet, certain roads reappear, and certain tragedies feel shaped instead of random.

In plain language, ka means fate, destiny, duty, and the wheel turning. People in Mid-World talk about it the way other settings might talk about providence or karma, but ka is less comforting. It can bring allies together, point toward the right road, or demand a painful sacrifice without explaining why.

That is why the game uses Ka Points as a mechanic. They are a small, playable way of showing that unseen forces are still pressing on the story.
