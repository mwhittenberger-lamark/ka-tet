# Ka-Tet: The Path of the Beam — Player Quickstart

Ka-Tet is a weird western horror-fantasy RPG about wanderers bound by fate, pulled across broken worlds to protect the Beams and decide whether destiny can be changed.

The tone is gritty, haunted, and strangely hopeful. Expect revolvers, ruined roads, psychic visions, broken machines, campfire confessions, impossible doors, and hard moral choices.

## What You Need to Know

You are part of a ka-tet: one made from many. Your group is bound by ka, the force of fate and destiny.

You do not need to have read The Dark Tower to play. The table will teach you the important ideas: ka, the Beams, thinnies, Todash space, gunslingers, the White, and the Red.

The setting’s big picture is easy to remember:

- The Dark Tower holds reality together.
- The Beams keep the Tower standing.
- The Crimson King and his servants want those supports broken.
- Your ka-tet is small, but fate has decided you matter.

## Core Roll

When the outcome is uncertain, roll:

d20 + ability modifier + proficiency if trained

Meet or beat the Difficulty Class set by the GM.

If your training, background, archetype, or gear clearly applies, add proficiency.

## Ka Points

Ka Points represent fate bending around you.

You can usually spend Ka to:

- Reroll a d20.
- Help another member of your ka-tet.
- Avoid catastrophe.
- Find a clue by fate.
- Push through a heroic moment.

Most characters begin with 1 Ka Point. You usually cannot hold more than 3 at once.

## What Players Do

In Ka-Tet, players:

- Travel dangerous roads through Mid-World and stranger places beyond it.
- Fight harriers, monsters, Low Men, and things from Todash.
- Protect fragile places, relics, and people caught in the Red’s shadow.
- Make hard choices when violence, duty, and mercy pull in different directions.
- Strengthen or fracture their ka-tet.
- Decide whether fate must be followed, bent, or broken.

## Core Terms

- **Ka:** fate, destiny, and the wheel turning.
- **Ka-tet:** a group bound by destiny strongly enough to change the story.
- **Beam:** one of the great forces holding worlds in balance.
- **Thinny:** a weak place in reality where the world starts sounding wrong.
- **Todash:** the unstable between-space reached through doors, visions, and disaster.
- **Gunslinger:** a knightly gunfighter of the old code.

## Before Your First Session

- Read this quickstart.
- Pick a pre-generated character or make one with the GM.
- Learn what your Ka Points do.
- Skim the firearms and combat rules if you expect a fight.
- Tell the GM your comfort level with horror.
- Bring dice if you have them.
