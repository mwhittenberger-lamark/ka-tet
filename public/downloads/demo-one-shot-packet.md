# Demo One-Shot Packet

## Demo Pitch

A dead railway line starts running again through a desert town that swears the tracks were torn up thirty years ago. The train carries six passengers, one locked door, and something in the baggage car that keeps singing in a dead child's voice.

## Runtime

3 to 4 hours.

## Player Count

4 to 6 players.

## Tone

Weird western horror, cosmic fantasy, hard choices, and gun smoke.

## What Players Need

No prior experience with Ka-Tet or The Dark Tower is required.

## Recommended Pre-Gens

- Avery "Stitch" Cole
- Naomi Blueglass
- Elias Turnpike
- Mercy-9

## Safety Note

This game may include horror, unreality, grief, violence, cults, and psychic trauma. The GM should discuss lines, veils, and pause tools before play.

## Structure

Act I: The train arrives.
Act II: The passengers reveal what they are hiding.
Act III: The locked door opens.
