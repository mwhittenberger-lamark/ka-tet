# Ka Points Cheat Sheet

Ka Points represent fate, destiny, luck, and the strange pressure of the Tower.

## Common Uses

Spend 1 Ka to:

- Reroll a d20 after seeing the result.
- Aid a ka-tet member when the bond matters.
- Notice a clue by fate, with GM approval.
- Push through fear, pain, or exhaustion for a heroic moment.

Spend 2 Ka to:

- Prevent a catastrophic outcome, with GM approval.
- Turn a lethal blow into survival at 1 HP.
- Keep the story from ending before ka is done with you.

## Earning Ka

The GM may award Ka when:

- You follow your destiny.
- You protect the ka-tet.
- You make a meaningful sacrifice.
- You face horror without turning away.
- A major story milestone is reached.

## Reminder

Ka is not mercy. Ka is motion.
