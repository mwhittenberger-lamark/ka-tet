# Combat Cheat Sheet

## Initiative

Roll:

d20 + Dexterity modifier

Highest result acts first.

## On Your Turn

You can usually:

- Move.
- Take one action.
- Use one bonus action if a feature allows it.
- Interact with one object.
- Take one reaction outside your turn if triggered.

## Common Actions

- Attack
- Dash
- Disengage
- Dodge
- Help
- Hide
- Ready
- Search
- Use Object

## Attacks

Roll:

d20 + ability modifier + proficiency if trained

If you meet or beat the target's AC, you hit.

## Damage

Roll the weapon or power damage, then add any listed modifier.

## Firearms

Track:

- Range
- Ammo
- Reload
- Cover
- Line of sight

## Downed Characters

When a character drops to 0 HP, follow the table's current death and injury rules. The GM may allow Ka to prevent a fatal result.
